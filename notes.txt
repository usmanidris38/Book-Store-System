        -Then change the information and click the <Update> button

    4. Details
        -If you want show the details about book then select any from the table and click <Details> button

        -Then change the information and click the <Update> button
        -If no need then cancel the process by clicking <Cancel> button

    5. Delete
        -If you want to delete any book information then first select any from the table and click <Delete> button
